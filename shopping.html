Looking for something new?

With multiple shopping malls, department stores filled with Danish fashion and design, different city quarters each with their own style and the pedestrian high street you'll find everything you ever wanted.

  • Experience an out of the ordinary shopping experience at Salling Department Store!
  • Save money while you shop!

Shopping Mall

  •

    Bruuns Galleri

    Denmark’s largest city-shopping centre housing over 100 shops offering groceries, fashion and dining.

  •

    Magasin

    An exclusive department store in the heart of Aarhus where you will find everything under one roof.

  •

    Storcenter Nord

    Sporting over 60 specialist outlets, a wide selection of restaurants and a play area for children.

  •

    Sailling

    The biggest shopping mall in Aarhus. With over 100 stores and open every day of the week this mall has everything.

Main Streets

  •

    Strøget

    No matter what you are looking for, you can be sure to find it here.

  •

    Vestergade

    Shop local at Vestergade, browse the stores and have a coffee break.

  •

    Volden

    Dont let this small street fool you, you'll find boutique shops far and wide.

  •

    Latin Quarter

    The oldest and most charming quarters in Aarhus. Chic shops and coffee await.

Top Stores

  •

    Badstuerock

    A vinyl record store that has been open since 1973, specialized in rock.

  •

    Prag

    Expanding to Aarhus, this vintage store sells clothes, accessories and more.

  •

    Bülow Duus

    Watch how glassblowing is done while also purchasing nice pieces of art.

  •

    Støy Munkholm

    Large high quality clothing store with unique brands for both men and women.
