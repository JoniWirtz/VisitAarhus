Join the fun!

There is always something going on in Aarhus and is known to be a city of music with a great variety of stages and genres. You can always join in to festivals all year round so don't be shy. Welcome to Aarhus!

Upcoming Events

  • The Bodyguard at Musikhuset

    Dec. 2nd - 13th

    One of the absolute iconic movies of the 1990’s has been made into a world-wide musical success. Feel or relive the thrill of The Bodyguard in December 2020, when it opens at Concert Hall Aarhus.

  • Christmas in Den Gamle By

    Nov. 21 - Jan. 3rd

    Treat yourself to a magical Christmas atmosphere and a wonderful nostalgia trip through four hundred years of Christmas history down The Old Town Museum's charming streets with horse-drawn carriages, Christmas decorations, and lit lanterns.

  • Christmas in Tivoli Friheden

    Dec 2nd - 13th

    Enjoy the most wonderful time of the year in the amusement park Tivoli Friheden when Santa Claus is coming to town. Experience the magic where Christmas lights, decorations and the normal rides set the scene for a magical Christmas day.

  • The Festival of Centuries

    Feb. 28th - Mar. 3th

    The Festival of the Century is a knowledge festival. With its mixture of knowledge, art, and culture, the festival each year turns the spotlight on a particular historical period to create a debate about the lives we live and the society around us.
