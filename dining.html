Satisfy your taste buds

A food revolution is sweeping Aarhus right now with new restaurants, cafes, microbreweries and provedores giving diners plenty to hunger over. From farmers markets to fancy-pants dining; food-truck parks, abundant nightlife and three Michelin stars granted restaurants.

  • Excite your tongue at one of these Michelin star restaurants!
  • Out late at night?

    Take a look at the nightlife here in Aarhus!

Resturaunts

  •

    Haervaerk

    Visit a restaurant where the commodities dictate the menu. Unpredictability, free hands and solid craft personified.

  •

    Moef

    Three different menus are presented to allow for some flexibility; Danish at heart and made with local produce.

  •

    Faour

    Faour is a restaurant in the heart of Aarhus, offering food from Levantine cuisine, or Mediterranean.

  •

    Frederikshøj

    Set in the former staff lodge to the Royal Palace, this restaurant is smart, luxurious and contemporary with a french touch.

Cafes

  •

    Cafe Viggo

    The center of cafe life for over 20 years. Serving a combination of traditional cafe food and French classics.

  •

    La Cabra Coffee

    A modern Coffee Roastery specialising in producing and serving a unique Nordic coffee experience

  •

    Caffe Ispirazione

    Eat as if you're in Italy. Caffe Ispirazione sources fresh ingredients from Italy creating authentic delicious food.

  • Bill's Coffee

    House roasted coffe, helpful staff and a lovely atmophere are waiting inside this coffee shop for someone to enjoy.

Local Favorites

  •

    Expresso House

    A thorough craft together with a generous dose of love provides a tasty and authentic product.

  •

    Street Food

    A permanent street food market, giving locals and tourists the opportunity to enjoy all various kinds of street food

  •

    French Corner

    The French Corner transports its customers to paris with their natural and fresh products.

  •

    Toast-it

    The ambition is to make the good old-fashioned toast, just in new clothes and with a new twist.
